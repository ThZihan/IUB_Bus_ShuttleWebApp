    Punctuality Percentage Rating Remarks

  • Total Trips: The total number of trips a shuttle has completed.
  • Average Occupancy: Average percentage of seats occupied during trips.
  • Maintenance Status:
    • Operational: Shuttle is fully functional.
    • Scheduled Maintenance: Routine servicing scheduled but currently operational.
    • Under Maintenance: Shuttle is not operational due to repairs.
